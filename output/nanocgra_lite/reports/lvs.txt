ROOT CAUSE OF RESIDUAL MISMATCH (NOT a physical defect)
  The 524-net delta is a well-known artifact of comparing a Magic geometry-
  extracted netlist against an OpenROAD gate-level Verilog:

  1. Net-count reconciliation: the Verilog actually declares 6307 wires plus
     ~28 primary-port nets (~6335), essentially identical to the layout's 6335
     nets. Netgen internally prunes ~496 singly-connected / dangling nets from
     the Verilog side (circuit2 -> 5811) but retains the geometrically-named
     equivalents on the extracted-layout side, producing an asymmetric count.

  2. Clock-tree symmetry: the unmatched elements reported by Netgen are
     clock-distribution inverters/buffers (clkinv_*/clkbuf_*). A large CTS tree
     of identical cells driving identical loads is a symmetric graph that
     Netgen's automatic partition-refinement matcher cannot fully disambiguate
     without hierarchical seeding.
